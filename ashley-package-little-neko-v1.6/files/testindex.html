A brief synopsis

Hi, my name is Jason, and I live at the intersection of technology and humanity. I'm a mobile developer, writer, entrepreneur, biohacker, and digital nomad. I explore the world while working from my laptop, training Martial Arts, having adventures, and pushing the boundaries of what it means to be human...all while growing and learning as much as humanly possible!

What I'm up to now

Back in April of 2012, I downsized my life by renting out my condo and moving into a 21' mini RV (VW Rialta)! I live and work from the road, developing software for my software company, while also freelance consulting on the side.
